## Style Prompt

Clean end-user presentation for ПРОкадры: calm, useful, practical, and easy to understand. The video should feel like a short product intro for employers and job seekers in procurement, not a technical audit. Use a light canvas, deep blue authority, cyan for smart search, and warm off-white surfaces.

## Colors

- Background: `#F4F8FB`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary text: `#0B1F33`
- Secondary text: `#49667D`
- Accent blue: `#1769E0`
- Accent cyan: `#17B8C8`
- Soft line: `#D7E4EE`

## Typography

- Headline: `Manrope`, 800-900 weight, tight tracking.
- Body and labels: `IBM Plex Sans`, 300-600 weight.
- Data chips: `IBM Plex Mono`, tabular numbers.

## Motion

- Medium-energy corporate explainer.
- Entrances should be precise and confident: y/x movement, scale, opacity, staggered cards.
- Transitions should be consistent: soft blue wipe with brief blur/crossfade.
- No chaotic glitch, no neon, no generic purple gradients.

## What NOT to Do

- No dark cyberpunk palette.
- No generic blue-purple gradient text.
- No tiny web-sized typography.
- No dense paragraph screens.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
- No server specs, infrastructure details, or engineering review language.
